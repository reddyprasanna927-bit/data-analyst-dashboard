# Project overview

An interactive Excel Dashboard designed to analyze sales performance using Pivot Tables, Charts, KPIs, and Slicers.

# (KPI) Questions
-What is the Total Sales Revenue?
-How many Units were Sold?
-What is the Average Sales per Transaction?
-What is the Monthly Sales Growth (%)?
-Which month generated the highest sales?
-Which city/region has the highest sales?
-Who is the top-performing salesperson?
-How are sales trending over time?

# process

-Developed a dynamic dashboard to provide actionable business insights.

# Project Insights
-Identified the top-performing products contributing the highest revenue.
-Analyzed monthly sales trends to track business growth and seasonal patterns.
-Evaluated salesperson performance to recognize top contributors.
-Compared sales across different locations to identify high-performing regions.
-Monitored key KPIs such as Total Revenue, Units Sold, and Average Sales.
-Enabled data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations and filters.

# Final Conclusion
This Excel Sales Dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations, KPI tracking, and dynamic filtering. It helps identify sales trends, top-performing products, regions, and salespersons, enabling faster and more informed business decisions. The dashboard demonstrates strong skills in data analysis, data visualization, and business reporting using Microsoft Excel
